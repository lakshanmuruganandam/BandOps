# Judge Q&A Prep

**Q: Why Band instead of just LangChain?**
A: Band is the interoperability layer. We needed agents built in different frameworks to collaborate securely with an immutable audit trail. Band provides the mesh that makes this possible.

**Q: Is the ledger really immutable?**
A: Yes, resolutions are cryptographically signed and stored in a write-only structure that acts as a system of record for FAA compliance.

**Q: Can this scale to other industries?**
A: Absolutely. The AOG use-case proves it works for high-stakes, regulated environments. This exact architecture can be deployed in healthcare, finance, or energy grid management.
