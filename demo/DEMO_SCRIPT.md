# BandOps Demo Script

## The 3-Minute Hackathon Demo

**[0:00 - 0:30] The Hook**
"When a commercial jet breaks down on the tarmac, it costs the airline $15,000 every single minute. Right now, fixing that involves mechanics, logistics, and flight ops shouting at each other over the phone. We built BandOps to change that."

**[0:30 - 1:15] The Platform**
"BandOps is a secure Multi-Agent Command Center powered by the Band API. Instead of human silos, we drop 6 specialized AI agents into a single Band-powered War Room."
*(Show the gorgeous UI, glassmorphism, dynamic data)*

**[1:15 - 2:00] The Action**
"Let's click 'Inject Chaos' to simulate an engine failure in Seattle. Immediately, the Line Mechanic agent diagnoses the fault. The Supply Chain agent jumps in—via the Band mesh—to find a replacement part in LA. The FAA Compliance agent strictly audits their plan to ensure it's legal."

**[2:00 - 2:30] Human Oversight**
"Band is the interoperability layer making this debate possible. The AOG Commander agent synthesizes the debate and presents a resolution to me, the human operator. I click Approve."

**[2:30 - 3:00] The Close**
"The decision is cryptographically logged to an immutable ledger for FAA compliance. We took a chaotic, multi-million dollar problem and solved it in seconds using Band. Thank you."
