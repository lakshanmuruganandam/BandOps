# BandOps Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title
**BandOps**
Multi-Agent Command Rooms for Regulated Crisis Operations.
*Powered by Band*

## Slide 2: The Problem
**Aircraft on Ground (AOG)**
- A grounded plane costs airlines **$15,000 per minute**.
- Mechanics, Logistics, Flight Ops, and the FAA are siloed.
- Communication is chaotic: emails, phone calls, whiteboards.
- Result: Hours of delays and millions lost.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**BandOps Command Center**
- We replace siloed human panic with an orchestrated swarm of specialized AI agents.
- The agents analyze faults, locate parts, and check regulations in seconds.
- **Human-in-the-loop**: Agents recommend, humans approve.

## Slide 4: Why Band?
**Band is the Interoperability Layer**
- We needed agents built in different frameworks to collaborate securely.
- Band provides the persistent room, the identity verification, and the audit trail.
- It is the mesh that makes enterprise-grade multi-agent systems possible.

## Slide 5: The War Room (Demo)
*Visual: Architecture and Agent Flow*
1. **Line Mechanic** diagnoses.
2. **Supply Chain** sources parts.
3. **Flight Ops** calculates delay costs.
4. **FAA Compliance** acts as an adversarial auditor.
5. **AOG Commander** synthesizes.

## Slide 6: Immutable Auditability
- In aviation, every decision must be auditable.
- BandOps logs the entire agent debate and human approval to an immutable ledger.
- Zero black boxes. Full traceability.

- Global Logistics (Supply Chain Shocks)
*Any high-stakes, regulated environment.*
